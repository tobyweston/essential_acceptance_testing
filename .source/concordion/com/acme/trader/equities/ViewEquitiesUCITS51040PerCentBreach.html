UCITS Compliant - 5% / 10% / 40% Rule (Happy Path)

Given

The following UCITS rules:

  • Each equity within the Asset Portfolio (AP) is not greater than 10% of AUM
  • All equities within the AP where the holding is greater than 5% of AUM then the total holding of these equities is less than or equal to 40% of AUM
