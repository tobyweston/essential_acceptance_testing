Get Assets via REST by Isin

Given

Equities exist, three of which have the Isin US38259P5089

Assets exist on two of these equities (id 0003571250 and 0205117919)

When

A user intends to find assets by Isin US38259P5089

Assets resource is queried with a HTTP GET on the URL... 

/apman/assets/isin/US38259P5089

with a "accept" mime-type of... 

application/json

Then

A list is returned, containing assets associated with equities 0003571250 and 0205117919
